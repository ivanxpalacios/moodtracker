About

Have you ever suffered from stress, anxiety, bad emotions, lack of sleep or something else similar? Would you like to feel more positive? Do you need a place to vent your thoughts? At Mood Tracker, we seek to be a self-care tool that helps you on your journey to address your emotional health needs and pay more attention to your emotions every day.

How to use

Enter the New Entry section when it is convenient for you at least once a day.
Enter the date, your general mood on that day, and how you feel about what happened that day. It is your space, write as much as you think necessary.
At the end, do not forget to include the things for which you are grateful on that day, being grateful helps us to value the small but good things that life has. For example: you liked the weather that day, you called your favorite family member, you ate something tasty, or you simply woke up feeling good.
